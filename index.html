How do we detect molecules?

Vast majority done at radio frequencies: rotational motions of molecules

Transition frequencies correspond with distribution of mass; $A(BC) \propto \frac{1}{I_{a(bc)}}$

Transition strength proportional to permanent dipole moment, $\mu_{a(bc)}$

Isomer, Isotopic, Chiral* specificity for mixtures

Need extremely accurate and precise catalogs of transitions

Where are the aromatics?

Ubiquitous in chemistry, but under-represented in space?

  • Lack of precise spectroscopic data
  • Small concentrations in space
  • Spectroscopically unfavorable (partition function, dipole moment)
  • Precursors to aromaticity
